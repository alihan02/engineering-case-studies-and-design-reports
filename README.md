# Mechanical Engineering Case Studies

A collection of mechanical design projects covering CAD, machine design,
structural analysis, mechanism design, and technical documentation.

## Projects

### Foldable Workshop Crane

A 2-ton hydraulic workshop crane designed and verified through structural
calculations, CAD assembly, technical drawings, and motion analysis.

[View project](./foldable-workshop-crane/)

---

### Two-Stage Industrial Gearbox

A 15 kW two-stage gearbox designed for speed reduction from 1750 rpm to
approximately 80–100 rpm, including gear, shaft, bearing, lubrication,
and housing design.

[View project](./two-stage-gearbox/)

---

### Automotive Scissor Jack

A 1.5-ton scissor jack designed in Siemens NX, including mechanism geometry,
power screw analysis, pin design, arm strength, and manufacturability considerations.

[View project](./scissor-jack/)
